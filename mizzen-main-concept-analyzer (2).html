Part 2: Idea Quality

Is this, subjectively, a good creative concept?

Does this idea feel fresh and distinctive? Or does it feel like something we (or competitors) have done before?
Is there a clear "aha" moment? Can you explain the core concept in one sentence that makes people lean in?
Does it have stopping power? Would this make someone pause their scroll, turn their head, or tell a friend?
Does this feel like us? Could this concept only come from our brand, or could any competitor do the same thing?
Can you picture our customer engaging with this? In real life, is this something our customer will think twice about?
If this were someone else's idea, would I wish we'd thought of it?
